Why Buy Motor Insurance?

Motor insurance gives protection to the vehicle owner against (i). damages to his/her vehicle and (ii). pays for any Third Party Liability determined as per law against the owner of the vehicle. Third Party Insurance is a statutory requirement. The owner of the vehicle is legally liable for any injury or damage to third party life or property caused by or arising out of the use of the vehicle in a public place. Driving a motor vehicle without insurance in a public place is a punishable offence in terms of the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988.

Add-Ons

Nil Depreciation – RTI is an add-on option which covers the gap between the Insured Declared Value and the invoice value of the car (Cover will be available upto 3yrs).

• For PC - The Insured will be given the option to choose the number of claims on differential rate against which the claims on nil depreciation add on cover will be paid up to maximum of specified number of times- 2,4 and unlimited.

• For TW and CV – 2 claims.

Return to Invoice Cover( For Total loss Claim ) – Covers depreciation amount of assessed damaged parts allowed for replacement during repairs in the event of partial loss ( Cover will be available up to 7yrs. for private cars, 5 years for TW and CV).

• Total Ex-Showroom Price - It includes Current Ex-Showroom Price as on Date of purchase and any other Accessories Price paid by Insured at the time of purchasing the Car and included in IDV.

• Road Tax - Road Tax amount! paid to RTO by Insured for the Car and included in IDV.

• Registration Charges - Registration charges paid by Insured to RTO for the Car and included in IDV.

• First Year Insurance Premium - Full First Year insurance premium paid for the Car and included in IDV

Additional Towing Charges – It allows reimbursement of towing charges in case of an admissible OD claim to the extent of Rs 10000/- over & above the normal policy limit of towing charges. (Can avail a total of towing charges of up to 11500/-. The cover will be available up to 7 yrs).

Engine Protection Cover – Covers the extension of damage (Consequential Damage) to internal parts of engine and gear box- arising out of water ingress, leakage of lubricant oil, etc. ( Cover will be available up to 5yrs).

• Damage lo Engine and Engine Parts and/or Gear Box and Gear Box Parts and/or Transmission or Differential Parts Assembly.

• This cover will also pay for the cost of lubricants oil/coolant lost due to leakage.

• Hybrid components like Hybrid battery, Inverter, Electric motor etc. will be covered.

Road Tax ( For Total loss Claim only ) – Extension provides protection against Road Tax that insured may have to pay while replacing the vehicle when a major accident to current vehicle (Cover shall be available up to 5yrs). IDV for road tax remain same for all renewals under this add on cover.

No Claim Bonus Protection Cover – Allows making a claim without the no-claim bonus getting reduced ( Cover will be available up to 7 yrs).

• Subject to the condition that the protection of NCB is limited to first two number of claims admissible and paid under the policy.

• No claim Bonus protection is allowed provided the policy is renewed within 90 days of the expiry date of the previous policy.

Consumable Expenses – Covers the cost of consumable which include nut and bold, screw ,washers, grease, lubricants clip, engine oil, gear box oil, power steering oil, coolant and items of similar nature excluding fuel (Cover will be available up to 5 yrs).

Roadside Assistance Cover – Roadside assistance including services like fuel assistance, towing facility, taxi benefit, accommodation benefit, battery jump start, flat tyre, mechanical/electrical repairs, breakdown support over phone.

• HO/ MTD has empaneled M/S Roadzen Assistance India Pvt Ltd as vendor for assistance.

High Value PA Cover – Under this cover , the company undertakes to indemnify the insured up to Rs 15 Lakhs per person (Maximum up to seating capacity of the vehicle) for death or total permanent disability(Cover will be available up to 7yrs).

Key Protection Cover – Cost of key replacement if the key is irrecoverable ( Cover will be available up to 5 yrs).

1.Repairs/Replacement of vehicle keys which are lost/misplaced/stolen.

2.When the vehicle lock is broken following burglary or theft attempt.

3.Damages to keys following an accident Amount payable: Cost of new lock/lock set and reasonable locksmith charges.

Personal Belongings Cover – Covers loss or damage to personal baggage while kept in the insured vehicle ( SI up to Rs 50000/-. Loss or damages to personal belongings including electronics items-Mobile , Laptop .. etc. & Cover will be available up to 7 yrs).

Tyre & Alloy Cover – Under this cover company undertakes to pay for repair and /or replacement of tyres damaged due to cut, burst, bulge, or continuous running under deflated condition (Cover will be available up to 3 yrs).

• There will be max 4 tyres and/ or 4 alloy /rims available for replacement in a policy term.

Additional Towing Charges – This Add On cover provides increased towing charges as it allows reimbursement of towing charges in case of an admissible OD claim to the extent of Rs. 10,000/- over & above the normal policy limit of towing charges. Thus the Insured can avail a total towing charges of upto Rs. 11,500/-. The cover will be available up to 7 years of the age of vehicle. Claim is limited to any two claims.

Loss of Contents Cover – Extension allows the insured to opt for SI up to Rs 20000/- with a 20% cap of SI for each individual item or actual value of lost item whichever is less for loss of contents in an accident.( Cover will be available up to 7 yrs).

Hybrid Protect Add on Cover – This Add On Cover indemnifies insured's expenses incurred for repair or replacement due to consequential damages arising out of water ingression/leakage of lubricating oil or coolant/moisture build up / mechanical shock / short circuit /unexpected power surge while charging the battery and damage to vehicle's under carriage arising out of any accidental external means, leading to loss or damage to the electric battery and / or electric motor and / or differential and transmission units and / or related HEV systems.

• In addition to protection to Hybrid components, this add on also covers consequential damage to IC Engine of Hybrid Vehicles. This Add On Cover is available only for Hybrid vehicles registered as private cars. This Add On Cover is available up to 3 years of age of vehicle. Maximum two claims are payable under this Add On Cover.

• This Add On will provide protection to Hybrid vehicles as under :

1.Basic Cover : It will cover the battery and hybrid components.

2.Comprehensive Cover : In addition to basic cover, this also includes coverage for the internal combustion engine.

Battery Protection – This Add On covers repair or replacement due to consequential damages arising out of water ingression/ moisture build up / short circuit leading to loss or damage to battery, drive Motor/Electric Motor, Battery Management System, Other Battery Electric Vehicle (BEV) systems, short circuit / unexpected power surge while charging the battery leading to damage to battery, motor or BEV systems.

• In addition to this, the damage to or theft of the charging cable while charging, which is attached to the vehicle or inside the vehicle will also be covered. Further transportation charges up to Rs. 5,000/- are also covered. The Insured can also opt to cover wall mountedchargersupplied by OEM with insuredvehicledue to loss by burglary, housebreaking or theft only, by paying additional premium. of 5% of Basic OD premium.
